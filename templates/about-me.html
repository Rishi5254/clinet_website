About Me

Dr. C. V. Siva Rama Prasad currently working as a assistant professor in St.Peter's Engineering College(Autonomous) ,Hyderabad. I earned Ph.D. from Acharya Nagarjuna University,India and Master's Degree from Structural Engineering at Gayatri Vidya Parishad College of Engineering, Visakhapatnam. Previously I worked in VBIT, Bulehora University, Ethiopia and Bhosho Architect Pvt.ltd.
